Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef, 1/2 onion, finely chopped, 1/2 cup shredded Colby Jack or Cheddar cheese, 1 teaspoon soy sauce, 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce, 1 egg, 1 (1 ounce) envelope dry onion soup mix, 1 clove garlic, minced, 1 tablespoon garlic powder, 1 teaspoon dried parsley, 1 teaspoon dried basil, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, 1/2 teaspoon crushed dried rosemary, salt and pepper to taste.

Direction

  1. Preheat a grill for high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, onion, cheese, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, egg, onion soup mix, garlic, garlic powder, parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Form into 4 patties.
  3. Grill patties for 5 minutes per side on the hot grill, or until well done. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.
