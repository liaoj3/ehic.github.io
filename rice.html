Ingredients

2 eggs, A bowl of white rice, Salt, Cauliflower oil.

Direction

  1. Break up the eggs.
  2. Turn stove to median and put cauliflower oil on fry pan.
  3. Put white rice and cook for 2 mintues.
  4. Turn stove to high and put cauliflower oil.
  5. Drizzle the eggs over.
  6. Put as much salt as you like.
